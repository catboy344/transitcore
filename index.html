The process

Three steps between you and your bus.

No app to download, no long queue at the park. Book from your hostel, get your ticket, travel.

01

Pick your route

Choose your destination and travel date — either on WhatsApp or at the campus desk. We'll confirm seat availability with GIGM in real time.

02

Pay & get your ticket

Pay at the desk or via transfer. You get a real ticket instantly — passenger details, QR code, seat and departure time, all on one slip.

03

Show up & travel

Arrive 30 minutes early with your ticket and valid ID. That's it — you're on your way, and we're a WhatsApp message away the whole trip.

Why TransitCore
